From its humble beginnings in the early 20th century, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad has forged an extraordinary legacy, transforming vision into impactful reality.
Established in 1926, this esteemed institution has stood tall for nearly a century, steadily evolving through the millennium. What began as the Indian School of Mines has blossomed into a full-fledged Indian Institute of Technology, operating with 18 vibrant branches across Engineering, Applied Sciences, Management Studies, and Humanities, cementing its place as a prominent global institution. This transformation marks a new era, building upon a centenary of outperforming achievements in both academia and industry.

Our campus is a hub of comprehensive learning, offering a diverse array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs. We foster a rich environment for research, leading to groundbreaking contributions. From pioneering the nation's first Geoarchaeology course to a unique integrated BTech-MBA program with IIM Mumbai, our academic offerings reflect a commitment to forward-thinking education. Faculty members are not just educators but innovators, with individuals like Prof Sekhar Dutta becoming Fellows of the Institution of Civil Engineers, UK, and Prof Anshumali shaping climate change policy. Our researchers, like the team that conducted the first-ever multiple-layered blasting using smart design, are at the forefront of industrial innovation.

IIT (ISM) Dhanbad shines on the global stage, proudly securing the 20th position worldwide in Engineering-Mineral and Mining in QS rankings, a significant leap and a testament to our specialized excellence. Our reach extends beyond the campus gates through vital community work, including skill development programs for local youth and leadership training for Panchayati Raj Functionaries. We embrace inclusivity, as demonstrated by the Uttar Pradesh government's support for Atul Kumar's IIT education following Supreme Court intervention, and our commitment to student well-being through initiatives like a fully operational campus creche. Our alumni, such as Ms Gargi Mishra, recognized among the top 100 inspiring women in mining globally, continue to make us proud. Even moments of campus joy, like the viral Diwali rocket video, add to the vibrant tapestry of student life.
As we continue to drive India's critical mineral mission and innovate in fields from Arctic MedTech to Hydrogen Valley development, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad remains dedicated to shaping future leaders and contributing to a brighter tomorrow.
